About Us

More data—such as paying attention to the eye colors of the people around when crossing the street—can make you miss the big truck - The Black Swan Man

Convex Intelligence Inc. (CI) is the brainchild of Strategy Consultant/Analytics Manager & Senior Data Scientist Prem and IT thespian cum Digital Marketing veteran Rajan. We specialize in delivering antifragile solutions to businesses making them not just resilient, but profitable in testing times. Philosopher, probabilist and flaneur Dr. Nassim Nicholas Taleb's seminal works on Convexity, Risk and Fragility form the foundation of our business model. One of the natural consequences of this approach is having Skin in the Game, which we value as our most important quality in delivering the best possible solutions. In addition to helping firms set up and scale Analytics Infrastructures efficiently, we also provide Strategic and Business Consulting solutions, Research and Product Development services. With a combined international experience of more than 35 years between us, we have accumulated a wealth of experience developing antifragile systems in domains such as Chemicals, Oil & Gas, Automotive, Defense, E-Commerce and Financial Technology, before cofounding Convex Intelligence Inc. It is privately owned and based in Chennai, India.

THE DEVIL IS IN THE QUANTITATIVE DETAILS

Oftentimes businesses are looking for that little nugget that would make them invincible in today's cut-throat environment. Large-scale data has become that goldmine to look for the elusive tidbit. But without sustained direction, subject matter expertise and quantitative finesse, it would be an expensive exercise in failure to find anything of interest that would improve profitability. More data does not necessarily mean better predictions. Consequently, many contemporary businesses struggle in transforming their enthusiastic early adoption of analytics platforms into scalable solutions.

Convex Intelligence can help you implement comprehensive data management/analysis solutions that overcome this transformation problem. With extensive experience in data mining, curation, method analysis, we have developed a range of problem specific data solutions, whose predictive capabilities are built on top of solid mathematical foundations of convexity. We understand the pitfalls that come with large datasets and attach a lot of value to avoid making sweeping judgments about their capabilities.
