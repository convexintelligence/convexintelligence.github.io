About Prem

Prem is a Management Consultant & Analytics Manager who values constant self-learning and training to challenge himself in providing only the best service to his clients. He has 15+ years experience working with a range of international organizations helping them address a variety of issues that require specialized research and quantitative focus. His clientele includes some of the largest companies/corporations with business interests in Chemicals, Oil & Gas, Automotive, Defense, E-Commerce and Financial Technology sectors. Prem and his team's solutions and recommendations have generated a collective revenue of more than a $1billion for his clients. He has advanced degress in Chemical Engineering and Mathematical Physics and attaches utmost importance to subject matter. He is a dedicated team player with excellent communication and persuasion skills. Prem speaks fluent English, German, Japanese, Hindi and Tamil and capable of having a basic conversation in Mandarin. He is also a self-taught avid coder with strong programming experience in C/C++, FORTRAN, Python, R, Perl and Haskell
